About the Registrar

The office oversees a wide range of services, including student registration, enrollment, encoding of grades and transcripts, issuance of official records, certifications, and diplomas, as well as the verification of academic credentials. It ensures that all academic data is properly documented and aligned with institutional policies and government regulations.
